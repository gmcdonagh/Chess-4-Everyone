Press R to reset your game. Press S to save your game.

This game is designed to teach people who need varying degrees of freedom to learn chess.

A limited player is a player that must play with a certain degrees of freedom and will use the mover and picker to play the chess game. An unlimited player has no limitation on how many degrees of freedom. Therefore, they play the game using their mouse to drag and drop the chess pieces.

Degrees of freedom indicates how many moves are allowed for a limited player. For example, one degree of freedom is allowed one move, two degrees two, and three degrees three.
